Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist
DO'S
• Use Privacy Settings: Regularly audit your account settings to control who sees your posts.
• Think Before You Post: Consider if you would be comfortable with a future employer or your grandmother seeing the content.
• Network Professionally: Use platforms like LinkedIn to connect with industry professionals and alumni.
• Verify Information: Fact-check articles or news before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
• Keep it Positive: Use your platform to highlight your achievements, volunteer work, and projects.
DON'TS
• Don't Post Disparaging Remarks: Avoid venting about professors, classes, or current/past employers.
• Don't Share Illegal Activities: Never post photos or text documenting underage drinking, drug use, or other illegal acts.
• Don't Engage with Trolls: Avoid public arguments or "flame wars" that can make you look unprofessional.
• Don't Over-Share Personal Info: Avoid posting your home address, phone number, or daily schedule/location in real-time.
• Don't Post During Class/Work: It shows a lack of focus and respect for your current commitments.
